# artifact-types.md
## Artifacts produced by curriculum-mapping

All outputs verify full standards coverage and carry the metadata block.

### Curriculum map
- **Purpose:** the big-picture view of units across a span with standards and big ideas.
- **Required elements:** units in sequence; standards per unit (verified); essential questions / big
  ideas; assessment checkpoints; a coverage check (every required standard appears once).

### Pacing guide
- **Purpose:** time allocation across the calendar.
- **Required elements:** units mapped to weeks/days; instructional + assessment + buffer days;
  realistic totals against the available calendar.
- **Template:** `assets/templates/pacing-guide-template.md`.

### Scope & sequence
- **Purpose:** the ordered list of skills/standards and the logic of their order.
- **Required elements:** ordered skills/standards; prerequisite → dependent rationale (vertical
  coherence); cross-subject connections (horizontal) where relevant.

## Validation (extends `shared/quality/verification-checklists.md`)
- **Coverage:** every required standard for the span is placed exactly once (no gaps/redundancy).
- **Pacing realism:** day/week totals fit the actual calendar with buffer.
- **Coherence:** prerequisites precede dependents; standards are real + verified.
