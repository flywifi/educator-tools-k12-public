# artifact-types.md
## Artifacts produced by family-communication

All outputs use placeholder names, plain language, and carry the metadata block + a "personalize
here" note.

### Class newsletter
- **Purpose:** regular update to all families on learning and logistics.
- **Required elements:** what we're learning (plain language); key dates; how to help at home; contact
  line. Skimmable sections.
- **Template:** `assets/templates/newsletter-template.md`.

### Parent/guardian letter or email
- **Purpose:** a focused message (welcome, unit intro, event, general update).
- **Required elements:** clear purpose; warm respectful tone; any action needed + deadline; contact.

### Conference talking points
- **Purpose:** help a teacher prepare for a family conference.
- **Required elements:** strengths-first structure; general prompts to discuss growth + goals;
  placeholders where the teacher inserts specifics; collaborative tone.

### Progress-report narrative comments
- **Purpose:** reusable, personalizable comment stems.
- **Required elements:** strengths-based stems by area; clearly marked blanks for the teacher to
  personalize; no auto-filled real data.

## Validation (extends `shared/quality/verification-checklists.md`)
- **No real student data**; placeholders only; "personalize here" marked.
- Plain language, jargon-free, ~grade 6-8 readability; translation-friendly.
- Respectful, strengths-based, culturally inclusive tone.
