# Beacon Charter Academy — Instructional SOP (sample)

> Placeholder/sample SOP for a fictional charter school in Orange County, FL. Public/sample content
> only; no real student or staff data. Used by `tools/context_demo.py` to demonstrate the
> upload → read → resolve-context → adapt loop.

## School profile
- Type: public **charter** school (independent governing board; sponsor: Orange County Public Schools)
- Schedule: **block schedule**, 90-minute periods
- Instructional model: **blended** (in-class + LMS)

## Operating procedures
1. **Pacing:** follow the school's block pacing guide; one standard cluster per 90-minute block.
2. **Grading:** **standards-based grading**, 1–4 scale (not points/percentages).
3. **Common assessment:** a **weekly common formative assessment** per course team, aligned to the
   week's B.E.S.T. benchmark(s).
4. **UDL:** every lesson includes at least one UDL option for representation, action/expression, and
   engagement.
5. **Family contact:** contact a family within **24 hours** of any grade of 1 (or D/F equivalent).
6. **Governance:** instructional changes that affect the charter contract go to the governing board.
